About Us

Our company is based on the belief that our customers cats needs are of the utmost importance. Our entire team is committed to meeting those needs. As a result, a high percentage of our business is from repeat customers and referrals. We sell to our customers directly, and also wholesale to fabulous pet stores nation-wide.

We do make refillable cat toys, but the original design is built to smell good to your cats for about a year, making it a great buy!
